Project Info

Black Bird Clothing is a personal project.
The website was designed more as a boutique then a store, having a smaller selection of products then most online stores.With this ideea in mind I made the navigation of the website a very fast and efficient one, having all the products on the same page but with a very simple and elegant way to to show only the category you want to see.
The color scheme of the website is also very simple resuming to just three colors: black and two shades of white that help you distinguish the menu from the rest of the page in a very subtle but efficient way.
