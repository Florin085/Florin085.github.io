Project Info

Climber is a simple concept of iOS app design that was created for people who are passionate about climbing and hiking.
The app helps you find the best locations sorting them by difficulty, distance or rating. The app also helps you find guides to help you through your adventure, and also helps you arrange meatups to find other persons with the same passions as you.
